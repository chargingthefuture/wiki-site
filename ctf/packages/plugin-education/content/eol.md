## What is End-of-Life Planning?
End-of-life planning helps you make important decisions about your health, assets, and wishes for the future. It ensures your voice is heard, even if you cannot speak for yourself.

## Why is it Important?
- Protects your wishes
- Reduces stress for loved ones
- Helps avoid legal complications
