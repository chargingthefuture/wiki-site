# Trauma-Informed Design Notes (EOL Plugin)

- Educational content is always optional and skippable.
- Users can pause and resume at any step; no forced completion.
- Clear, gentle language and warnings before emotionally heavy sections.
- No judgment on unconventional choices or minimal assets.
- No data is stored or shared without explicit user action.
- Crisis resource links are available throughout the flow.
- Interface avoids triggering visuals or language.
